# Draft Email to Emil — Club President

**Subject:** Volunteering for Website & Newsletter — Otter Valley R&G

---

Hi Emil,

I hope you're doing well. I wanted to reach out about something I've been thinking about for a while now.

I've been a member at Otter Valley for about 10 years, and the club has given me a lot over that time. I'd like to give something back.

I understand the club is currently without someone to handle the newsletter, and I know the website could use some attention too. I'd like to volunteer my services for both. I work professionally with web development and digital content, and I'd be happy to put those skills to work for the club — no cost to Otter Valley.

Here's what I have in mind:

**Website:**
- A complete rebuild of the club's website with a modern, professional design
- Video content showcasing our facilities — the trap fields, rifle range, sporting clays course, archery range, and fishing pond
- An interactive events calendar, live range conditions, and competition results/leaderboards
- A dedicated CAN-AM page that gives the event the spotlight it deserves
- Links to our affiliate organizations (CCFR, NFA, OFAH, and HuntFishOntario)
- The kind of online presence that puts Otter Valley on par with — or ahead of — any gun club in Ontario

**Newsletter:**
- Pick up where things left off and get a regular newsletter going again
- Keep members informed about upcoming events, range updates, competition results, and club news

I've already started putting together a preview of what the new site could look like. I'd love to show it to you and the board when it's ready — no commitment needed, just a look at what's possible.

Down the road, if there's ever an opportunity to get more involved with the board, that's something I'd be interested in as well. But for now, I'm happy to contribute wherever the club can use the help.

Would you be open to chatting about this? I'm available whenever works for you.

Thanks Emil, and I'll see you at the club.

Mike Kapin
